What language is this shit taken from?

"Lorem ipsum" is a type of pseudo-Latin—it's not real Latin, but it's based on Latin words. The standard text comes from a scrambled version of a passage from Cicero's work "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum", written in 45 BC.

It was altered and jumbled over time to make it look like readable text without being meaningful, so it wouldn’t distract readers—perfect for placeholder text in publishing and design.
